BOTTOM LINE

Originally created as a university project and fueled by a shared passion for games.


Bottom Line is a choice-based video game (visual novel) centered on the theme of global warming. The game begins with a cinematic scene that establishes the atmosphere and the story. Set in a fictional apocalyptic future caused by the consequences of climate change, the player’s goal is to survive in a hostile world. The protagonist, suffering from amnesia, awakens in a mysterious underground environment where a few scattered rations can be found. As the adventure progresses, the character must confront a dangerous world and strange creatures that appeared during the global catastrophe. Throughout the game, the player gradually uncovers the truth behind the apocalypse and the protagonist’s identity, slowly revealing the events that led to their current situation.

Through this website, divided into three pages, each representing a different field of work, we aim to share our development process with you.
