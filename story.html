STORY TELLING

The core and base of our game project.

The Environment

A world reeling from climate change sets the stage for our story. Humanity’s actions have left the Earth’s surface fragile and unforgiving, while the underground laboratories of Nex Gen Corp operate in secrecy, pushing the boundaries of science to “restore” what has been damaged — and in doing so, creating unforeseen consequences.

Characters

Michael Silas – CEO of Nex Gen Corp. Greedy and reckless, his decisions brings catastrophe.
Dr. Seraphine Vale – Head scientist of the environmental department, driven by her ambition to restore the planet. Brilliant, determined, and unafraid to confront authority.
The Melted Child – a child affected by the virus that would, depending on the player's choices, follow and therefore accompany them.

Nex Gen Corp

A secretive biotech company, Nex Gen Corp is at the heart of the narrative. Their mission is to experiment on nature and combat climate change, but their methods are morally ambiguous. Their labs, hidden deep underground, are where innovation collides with danger, and where one mistake can spiral into disaster.

Monsters

The experiments on mutated praying mantises and other unnatural combinations have resulted in terrifying creations: The Grinwalker, a monstrous being with a haunting visage. In the meantime a virus spread and affect human beings changing them into melted creatures that suffer eternally.

Story Structure

The narrative unfolds through three distinct stages: an initial phase where players discover the game’s world and its environment, a second phase where the story deepens and the mysteries behind the events begin to reveal themselves, and a final stage where the player’s journey leads to one of the possible endings.

The Plot/ Conflict

Dr. Vale’s dual projects — “M4-15” and “S4V3” — collide catastrophically due to sabotage and mismanagement. A reckless CEO decision combined with unforeseen risks leads to a massive lab explosion, destroying the underground facility and devastating the Earth’s protective ozone layer. Humanity’s arrogance has unleashed both environmental ruin and monstrous beings into the world.

Call To Action

The events of this story are fictional, but the environmental crisis that inspired it is real. By exploring a world shaped by climate change and human ambition, we hope players will reflect on the fragile balance of our planet and the importance of protecting the environment before it is too late.
